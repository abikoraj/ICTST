About Us

ITCST is a student-run club in Mahendra Morang Adarsha Multiple Campus , Biratnagar. It is looked upon by a group of creative and tech enthusiastic students. Our objectives include organizing a wide array of sessions, workshops and training programs. Our aim pursuits opportunities to help students in all possible ways to set an effort to point students to the right direction.
